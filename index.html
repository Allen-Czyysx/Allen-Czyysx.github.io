About Me

I am Zihao Chen. I am a Ph.D. student at East China Normal University, majoring in data science and engineering. I have been conducting research on distributed machine learning systems. My Ph.D. supervisors are Chen Xu and Aoying Zhou.

In the past, I received my Bachelor's degree of science in mathematics from East China University of Science and Technology.
